##  Lab Summary — Internet Protocol Troubleshooting Commands  


This lab introduced basic tools used to check whether a computer can reach other systems on the internet. I practiced three types of checks:

- **Layer 3:** Used commands like `ping` and `traceroute` to see if a destination is reachable and how traffic travels to it.  
- **Layer 4:** Used `netstat` and `telnet` to view active connections and confirm whether a specific port is open or blocked.  
- **Layer 7:** Used `curl` to send a simple web request and confirm that a website responds correctly.

Overall, the lab helped me understand how to verify connectivity step‑by‑step, from basic reachability to application‑level responses.
